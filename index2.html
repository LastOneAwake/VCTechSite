About

Who We Are • Why we do it

The Technology Club is the point of convergance for a variety of technology focused disciplines at Valencia College.
Though our focus is often on programming, networking, or general IT, we welcome anyone interested from any field of study. Our goal is to build a community through which students can grow and prosper, learning from one another and through the club sponsored Tech Talks.
We host speakers from various industries, provide weekly study sessions for those who might need a little extra help, and are expanding into providing IT services for Valencia students at a significantly lower cost than might be found elsewhere.
This is who we are.

A career in technology can mean any one, or more, of an ever growing plethora of things. Not only that, technology isn't exactly known for remaining stagnant. Instead we're all part of the most constantly and consistently evolving industry in the world.
This can seem daunting, intimidating even.
In response to that our club seeks to build a communiy for students.
One where we can come together and learn from one another, in doing this we not only begin to build a network of like-minded individuals but develop the kind of connections that are needed in this undustry.
All the things we do we do to inspire, to learn, and to educate.
We're here to share our love of technology with others like us.
